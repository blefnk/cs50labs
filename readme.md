## Academic Honesty

The course‚Äôs philosophy on academic honesty is best stated as ‚Äúbe reasonable.‚Äù The course recognizes that interactions with classmates and others can facilitate mastery of the course‚Äôs material. Collaboration on problem sets is not permitted except to the extent that you may ask classmates and others for help so long as that help does not reduce to another doing your work for you. Collaboration on the course‚Äôs quiz is not permitted at all. Collaboration on the course‚Äôs final project is permitted to the extent prescribed by its specification.
